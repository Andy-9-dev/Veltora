DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The redesign achieves "warm, prestigious, unmistakably African luxury" through:

1. WARM COLOR FOUNDATION
   • Off-white, sand, taupe create sophisticated, inviting base
   • Replaces cold grays and stark whites

2. RICH GOLD ACCENTS
   • Replaces black with prestigious gold (#B5892A)
   • Creates visual prestige and warmth throughout

3. ELEGANT TYPOGRAPHY
   • Playfair Display conveys luxury and elegance
   • Inter provides clean, modern utility

4. SUBTLE SOPHISTICATION
   • Glass-morphism, warm shadows, directional gradients
   • Adds depth without harshness

5. CONSISTENT VISUAL LANGUAGE
   • Gold accents applied uniformly
   • Pale gold icon backgrounds throughout
   • Warm gradients for all overlays
